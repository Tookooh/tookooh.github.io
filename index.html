Who We Are

Just a bunch of
surf lovers

Mahatun Surf School is a small, family-run spot right on Dewata Beach, tucked between the old port town of Galle and the laid-back village of Unawatuna. We've been out here for years, and we still love every session just as much as the first.


Whether you've never been on a board before or you just want to get more comfortable in the water, we'll take care of you. No pressure, no rush — just good waves and good people.

What We Offer

Sessions & Hire

Everything comes with gear and a safety chat before you get in. Prices are per person unless it says otherwise.

🏄

Beginner Surf Lesson

Never surfed before? No worries. We'll start on the sand, get you comfortable, then walk you into the water together. Most people are up on their feet within the first session.

USD $35 / 2 hrs
  • Soft-top board & leash
  • Rash guard included
  • Theory + beach drills
  • In-water coaching
🌊

Intermediate Session

Can you ride the whitewash but want to get better? We'll spend the session working on turning, timing, and picking your waves a bit smarter. Relaxed and practical.

USD $50 / 2.5 hrs
  • Performance shortboard option
  • Video analysis
  • Break selection guidance
  • Max 4 students per guide
🤙

Private Coaching

Just you and one of our guys for a full 2 hours. Great if you want to focus on something specific, or just prefer learning without a group around.

USD $80 / 2 hrs
  • Full equipment selection
  • Video & dryland coaching
  • Personalized drill plan
  • Follow-up notes & tips
🛹

Board Hire

Know what you're doing and just need a board? We've got shortboards, longboards, eggs, and funboards ready to go. All waxed up and in good shape.

USD $12 / half day
  • Full-day rate: $20
  • Leash & wax included
  • ID deposit required
  • Fin adjustments on request
📦

3-Day Surf Camp

Three days of surfing, hanging out on the beach, and eating well. Morning sessions, some beach yoga, and simple home-cooked meals. Easy and fun.

USD $180 / person
  • 6 surf sessions total
  • Accommodation (twin share)
  • Meals & coconut water
  • Yoga & meditation class
📸

Surf Photography

We'll have someone in the water taking photos of your session. You get a folder of edited shots the same day — nice to have as a memory, or to show people back home.

USD $30 / session
  • 30+ edited digital photos
  • Drone shots available +$15
  • Delivered via Google Drive
  • Print-ready resolution
